Research topics

  • Survival analysis with an emphasis on:
    • Interval censored data
    • Recurrent events data
    • Competing events/multi-state models
    • Cure models
    • Pseudo-regression
  • Regularisation methods: fused-Lasso, adaptive-ridge
  • Machine learning: super learner, computational optimal transport, sum product networks.
